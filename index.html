About Me

I'm Muhammad Haziq Imran — an AI Engineer from Rahim Yar Khan, Pakistan, graduating with a Bachelor's in Artificial Intelligence (Class of 2026). I'm the kind of person who genuinely gets excited reading AI papers at midnight and then immediately wants to build something with what I just learned.

My journey into AI started with curiosity about how machines can understand language, and it quickly grew into a deep passion for building intelligent systems that actually work in the real world — not just in theory. Over four years I've gone from writing my first neural network to designing full multi-agent pipelines with LangGraph, RAG architectures with vector databases, and real-time APIs that serve live users. Outside of building, I'm driven by a simple belief: AI should work for everyone, including Urdu speakers in Pakistan. That's why I build bilingual, accessible AI tools. My FYP ShopGen — a LangGraph WhatsApp shopping assistant — reflects that mission perfectly. I'm actively looking for my first professional AI Engineer role where I can contribute, grow fast, and keep building things that matter.
